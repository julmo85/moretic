Accessibility project in Fribourg

MoreTIC is also developing Architectural Autonomy Without Borders, a practical field guide to better document real-life accessibility in Fribourg.

The idea is to gather existing information, identify practical gaps and prepare pilot sheets on routes, accessible toilets, disabled parking spaces, cultural venues and everyday obstacles.
